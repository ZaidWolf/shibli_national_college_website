About Shibli Nomani From Wikipedia

Shibli Nomani (3 June 1857 – 18 November 1914, Azamgarh district) was an Islamic scholar from Indian subcontinent during British Raj.[1] He was born at Bindwal in Azamgarh district of present-day Uttar Pradesh. He is known for the founding the Shibli National College in 1883 and the Darul Mussanifin in Azamgarh.
